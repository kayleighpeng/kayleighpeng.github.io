project overview.

Northwestern Formula Racing Ergonomics.

The ergonomic components of NFR22, Northwestern Formula Racing's 2021-2022 car, include the dashboard, seat (pictured left), floors, firewall & headrest. Together, these components ensure the comfort and safety of the driver.

design process.

Needfinding

  • Interviewed past NFR drivers and ergonomic project leads to determine design direction.

Prototyping

  • Created various CAD models of different ergonomic components using SolidWorks and laser-cut applicable prototypes (pictured right).

Design Review

  • Presented design direction of ergonomic components to stakeholders for review and feedback.

Iteration

  • Updated designs based on design review feedback, and repeated the entire process to improve overall design.

design features.

Seat

  • Comfort: the seat was molded to an actual driver, making the seat's contours close to those of a human's body.

Dashboard

  • Road visibility: the top of the dashboard followed the contour of the frame more closely for easier visibility of the road for drivers.

Firewall

  • Easier placement: the inserts of the firewall (pictured left) are pins for easier alignment and placement of the firewall.
